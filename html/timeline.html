Booking appointments

8 - 12 weeks

This appointment officially books you in for maternity care. It's best to have it by the time you are 10 weeks pregnant.

Please ensure you have completed this step
Jan 18

Dating Scan

10 - 14 weeks

This is your first ultrasound scan. Its purpose is to give you a more accurate due date. You will be offered optional screening tests during pregnancy to try to find health problems that could affect you or your baby.

Jan 24

16 week appointment

16 weeks

Your midwife will give you information about the ultrasound anomaly scan you will be offered at 18-20 weeks.

Feb 14

Anomaly Scan

18 - 20 weeks

The purpose of this scan is to check the physical development of your baby and make sure that there are no physical abnormalities.

Feb 18

25 week appointment

25 weeks

If this is your first baby, you will have an appointment to check your blood pressure, check your urine for protein and measure the size of your uterus.

Feb 26

28 week appointment

28 weeks

At this appointment you will be offered vaccines and more screening tests in addition to routine health checks.

Feb 26

31 week appointment

31 weeks

If this is your first baby, you will have an appointment to check your blood pressure, review the results of screening tests and measure the size of your uterus.

Feb 26

34 week appointment

34 weeks

At this appointment your midwife will give you information on preparing for birth, including how to know when you are in labour, ways of coping with pain and your birth plan.

Feb 26

36 week appointment

36 weeks

At this appointment your midwife will give you information on caring for your baby and looking after your own health after the birth, as well as routine health checks.

Feb 26

38 week appointment

38 weeks

You will have some routine health checks and discuss the choices you will be offered if your pregnancy lasts longer than 41 weeks.

Feb 26

40 week appointment

40 weeks

If this is your first baby, you will have an appointment to discuss what happens if your pregnancy lasts longer than 41 weeks and do some routine health checks.

Feb 26

41 week appointment

41 weeks

At this appointment you will have some routine checks, discuss the options and choices for induction of labour and offer you a membrane sweep.

Feb 26

42 week appointment

42 weeks

If you have not had your baby by 42 weeks and have chosen not to have an induction, you should be offered increased monitoring of the baby.
