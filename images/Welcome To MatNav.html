To get a Midwife, you must be registered with a General Practitioner.

What is a Midwife?

A midwife is a nurse, who is trained to assist women in childbirth.

What is a General Practitioner?

A General Practitioner (GP) is a medical doctor who treats acute and chronic illnesses. If you are currently within the UK, you are entitled to register with a GP in your area

You must go for a first appointment

If you're due date is while you are in the UK, and you would like to receive antenatal care, you must go to your GP. This may incur additional costs, so please ask your GP about where to get more information, it is your right to know these costs.
